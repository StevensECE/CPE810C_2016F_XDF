This is a guide file for other team to send data to us.

Content:
<n>:new line;
<stylechange>:change a style of text;

Font:
sample:languageChinesecolorbluefonttimessize28bold0italic0underline0end<stylechange>
This means:language(English/Chinese)+color(color of text, such as red blue etc.)+font(font of text, such as times etc.)+size(color of text, such as 12.0 etc.)+bold(bool,0 or 1)+italic(bool,0 or 1)+underline(bool,0 or 1)+end<stylechange>.
